Step-by-Step Interactive Process

  1. Load KID Content: Fritz > Database > Select Powerbook 2026 or OpeningEncyclopaedia_2025.cbh (in ChessBase/Bases folder). For Powerbook: Filter "King's Indian" (e4 Nf6...g6; or reversed for White). See tree: moves, % wins, transpositions. For Encyclopedia: Text > Ideas for Your Repertoire > Navigate to KID (e.g., "Indian Defences" > King's Indian > specific lines). Load games/articles.
  2. Train Lines (Quiz Mode): Right-click position > Replay/Test or Play Against Database Book. Fritz plays opponent moves from the book (weighted by stats—mainlines ~70%). You must find the best reply. Wrong? Hint/stats show (e.g., "13...a5: 62% wins"). Tracks progress/accuracy.
  3. Spar & Practice Games: New Game - Black/White from KID start. Fritz uses personalities (Hypermodern for Black KID fianchetto/flank attacks; Romantic for tactics). Or legends (Fischer/Tal play KID-like). Bullet mode for timed drills. Import Lichess PGNs for custom KID reps.
  4. Guidance & Feedback: AI Coach: Voice comments on 170+ themes (e.g., "Great ...Ng4 idea!"). Multilingual, adaptive. Visual tree/stats guide plans (e.g., "Avoid 9.Be3? 45% Black wins"). Replay variations instantly.
